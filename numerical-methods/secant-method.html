Secant Method

The method is based on the idea of finding a straight line that intersects the curve representing the equation in two points, and then determining the root of the equation as the intersection of this line with the x-axis.

Suppose \(x_0\) and \(x_1\) are two initial approximations. Then the equation of line joining the points \((x_0 , f(x_0))\) and \((x_1, f(x_1))\) is given by

\[ y = f(x_0) + \frac{f(x_1) - f(x_0)}{x_1 - x_0}(x - x_0) \tag{1} \]

The line (Equation (1)) intersects the x-axis at \((x_2, 0)\) where

\[ x_2 = x_1 - \frac{f(x_1)(x_1 - x_0)}{f(x_1) - f(x_0)} \]

In general, the iterative method is defined by,

\[ x_{n+1} = x_n - f(x_n) \cdot \frac{(x_n - x_{n-1})}{f(x_n) - f(x_{n-1})}, \quad n \geq 1 \]
  • Order of Convergence

    • Example 1

    Find the smallest positive real root of \(\cos(x) - x e^x = 0\), correct to three decimal places.

    Solution:

    Let \(x_0=0.5,\; x_1=0.6\)

    Let \(f(x) = \cos(x) - x e^x\)

    Substituting interval endpoints

    \[ f(0.5)=\cos(0.5)-0.5e^{0.5}\approx 0.0532 \] \[ f(0.6)=\cos(0.6)-0.6e^{0.6}\approx -0.2679 \]

    By Intermediate Value Theorem,

    \[ f(0.5) \cdot f(0.6) < 0 \]

    Hence, smallest positive root lies in \([0.5,\,0.6]\).

    For correct to three decimal places,

    \[ |x_{n+1} - x_n| \leq \frac{1}{2} \times 10^{-3} = 5 \times 10^{-4} \]

    In general, the iterative method is defined by,

    \[ x_{n+1} = x_n - f(x_n) \cdot \frac{(x_n - x_{n-1})}{f(x_n) - f(x_{n-1})}, \quad n \geq 1 \]

    For the first iteration of the secant method,

    \[ x_2 = x_1 - f(x_1) \cdot \frac{x_1-x_{0}}{f(x_1)-f(x_{0})}. \] \[ x_2 = 0.6 - f(0.6) \cdot \frac{0.6-0.5}{f(0.6)-f(0.5)}. \] \[ x_2 = 0.6 - (-0.2679) \cdot \frac{0.6 - 0.5}{-0.2679 - 0.0532} \] \[ x_2 \approx 0.5165 \] \[ |x_2 - x_1| = |0.5165 - 0.6| = 0.0835 \] \[ f(x_2) = \cos{0.5165}-(0.5165)e^{0.5165} \approx 3.821 \times 10^{-3} \]

    For the second iteration of the secant method,

    \[ x_3 = x_2 - f(x_2) \cdot \frac{x_2-x_1}{f(x_2)-f(x_1)}. \] \[ x_3 = 0.5165 - f(0.5165) \cdot \frac{0.5165-0.6}{f(0.5165)-f(0.6)}. \] \[ x_3 = 0.5165 - (3.821 \times 10^{-3}) \cdot \frac{0.5165-0.6}{(3.821 \times 10^{-3}) - (-0.26794)} \] \[ x_3 \approx 0.5176741 \] \[ |x_3 - x_2| = |0.5176 - 0.5165| = 1.1 \times 10^{-3} \] \[ f(x_3) = \cos{0.5176}-(0.5176)e^{0.5176} \approx 2.6575 \times 10^{-4} \]

    For the third iteration of the secant method,

    \[ x_4 = x_3 - f(x_3) \cdot \frac{x_3-x_2}{f(x_3)-f(x_2)}. \] \[ x_4 = 0.5176 - f(0.5176) \cdot \frac{0.5176-0.5165}{f(0.5176)-f(0.5165)}. \] \[ x_4 = 0.5176 - (2.6575 \times 10^{-4}) \cdot \frac{0.5176-0.5165}{(2.6575 \times 10^{-4}) - (3.821 \times 10^{-3})} \] \[ x_4 \approx 0.5176822 \] \[ |x_4 - x_3| = |0.5176741 - 0.5176822| = 8.1 \times 10^{-6} \] \[ |x_4 - x_3| < 5 \times 10^{-4} \]

    So we stop, and the root (to three decimal places) is \(0.5176822\)

    Example 2

    Find the root of the function \(f(x) = x^3 + 2x^2 - x - 1\) using the Secant method. Perform 3 iterations starting with \(x_0 = 0\) and \(x_1 = 1\).

    Solution

    Substituting interval endpoints

    \[ f(0)=(0)^3 + 2(0)^2 - (0) - 1 = -1 \] \[ f(1)=(1)^3 + 2(1)^2 - (1) - 1 = 1 \]

    By Intermediate Value Theorem,

    \[ f(0) \cdot f(1) < 0 \]

    Hence, smallest positive root lies in \([0,\,1]\).

    In general, the iterative method is defined by,

    \[ x_{n+1} = x_n - f(x_n) \cdot \frac{(x_n - x_{n-1})}{f(x_n) - f(x_{n-1})}, \quad n \geq 1 \]

    Iteration 1

    \[ x_2 = x_1 - f(x_1) \cdot \frac{x_1-x_{0}}{f(x_1)-f(x_{0})}. \] \[ x_2 = 1 - f(1) \cdot \frac{1-0}{f(1)-f(0)}. \] \[ x_2 = 1 - (1) \cdot \frac{1 - 0}{1 - (-1)} \] \[ x_2 \approx 0.5 \] \[ |x_2 - x_1| = |0.5 - 1| = 0.5 \] \[ f(x_2) = (0.5)^3 + 2(0.5)^2 - (0.5) - 1 = -1.875 \]

    Iteration 2

    \[ x_3 = x_2 - f(x_2) \cdot \frac{x_2-x_1}{f(x_2)-f(x_1)}. \] \[ x_3 = 0.5 - f(0.5) \cdot \frac{0.5-1}{f(0.5)-f(1)}. \] \[ x_3 = 0.5 - (-1.875) \cdot \frac{0.5-1}{-1.875 - 1} \] \[ x_3 \approx 0.8260 \] \[ |x_3 - x_2| = |0.8260 - 0.5| = 0.326 \] \[ f(x_3) = (0.8260)^3 + 2(0.8260)^2 - (0.8260) - 1 = 0.1021 \]

    Iteration 3

    \[ x_4 = x_3 - f(x_3) \cdot \frac{x_3-x_2}{f(x_3)-f(x_2)}. \] \[ x_4 = 0.8260 - f(0.8260) \cdot \frac{0.826-0.5}{f(0.8260)-f(0.5)}. \] \[ x_4 = 0.5176 - 0.1021 \cdot \frac{0.826-0.5}{0.1021-(-1.875)} \] \[ x_4 \approx 0.8091 \] \[ |x_4 - x_3| = |0.8260 - 0.8091| = 0.0169 \] \[ f(x_4) = (0.8091)^3 + 2(0.8091)^2 - (0.8091) - 1 = 0.0298 \]
