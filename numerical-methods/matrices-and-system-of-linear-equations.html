Matrices and system of linear equations

We will be solving system of linear equations of the form

\[ Ax = b \]

where \(A\) is a \(n \times n\) matrix of coefficients and \(b\) is a vector of constants with length \(n\). We solve for the unknown \(x\).

For a unique solution to exist, we assume \(det(A) \neq 0\).

We have again two types of method:

1. Direct methods

2. Iterative methods.
