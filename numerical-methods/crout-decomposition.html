Crout's Decomposition

\[ A = LU \]

where, \(U\) is a unit upper trianglar matrix, \(|U| = 1\) and \(L\) is lower trianglar matrix, \(|L| = \ell_{11}\ell_{22} \ldots \ell_{nn} \ne 0\).
