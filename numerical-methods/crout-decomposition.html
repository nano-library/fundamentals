Crout's Decomposition

\[ A = LU \]

where, \(U\) is a unit upper trianglar matrix, \(|U| = 1\) and \(L\) is lower trianglar matrix, \(|L| = \ell_{11}\ell_{22} \ldots \ell_{nn} \ne 0\).

Example 1

Find the Crout's Decomposition of the following matrix.

\[ A= \begin{bmatrix} 6 & -2 & 2 & 4 \\ 12 & -8 & 6 & 10 \\ 3 & -13 & 9 & 3 \\ -6 & 4 & 1 & -18 \end{bmatrix} \]

Solution

In Crout's factorization, we write

\[ A = LU \]

where \(L\) is lower triangular (with non-unit diagonal) and \(U\) is upper triangular with unit diagonal.

\[ A= \begin{bmatrix} \ell_{11} & 0 & 0 & 0\\ \ell_{21} & \ell_{22} & 0 & 0\\ \ell_{31} & \ell_{32} & \ell_{33} & 0\\ \ell_{41} & \ell_{42} & \ell_{43} & \ell_{44} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & u_{12} & u_{13} & u_{14}\\ 0 & 1 & u_{23} & u_{24}\\ 0 & 0 & 1 & u_{34}\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ {\tiny LU= \begin{bmatrix} \ell_{11} & \ell_{11}u_{12} & \ell_{11}u_{13} & \ell_{11}u_{14}\\ \ell_{21} & \ell_{21}u_{12}+\ell_{22} & \ell_{21}u_{13}+\ell_{22}u_{23} & \ell_{21}u_{14}+\ell_{22}u_{24}\\ \ell_{31} & \ell_{31}u_{12}+\ell_{32} & \ell_{31}u_{13}+\ell_{32}u_{23}+\ell_{33} & \ell_{31}u_{14}+\ell_{32}u_{24}+\ell_{33}u_{34}\\ \ell_{41} & \ell_{41}u_{12}+\ell_{42} & \ell_{41}u_{13}+\ell_{42}u_{23}+\ell_{43} & \ell_{41}u_{14}+\ell_{42}u_{24}+\ell_{43}u_{34}+\ell_{44} \end{bmatrix} } \]

Comparing entries with \(A\), we compute the unknowns in order.

Step 1: First row

\[ a_{11}=\ell_{11}\implies \ell_{11}=6 \] \[ a_{12}=\ell_{11}u_{12}\implies u_{12}=\frac{a_{12}}{\ell_{11}}=\frac{-2}{6}=-\frac{1}{3} \] \[ a_{13}=\ell_{11}u_{13}\implies u_{13}=\frac{a_{13}}{\ell_{11}}=\frac{2}{6}=\frac{1}{3} \] \[ a_{14}=\ell_{11}u_{14}\implies u_{14}=\frac{a_{14}}{\ell_{11}}=\frac{4}{6}=\frac{2}{3} \]

Step 2: First column

\[ a_{21}=\ell_{21}\implies \ell_{21}=12 \qquad a_{31}=\ell_{31}\implies \ell_{31}=3 \qquad a_{41}=\ell_{41}\implies \ell_{41}=-6 \]

Step 3: Second column of \(L\) and second row of \(U\)

\[ a_{22}=\ell_{21}u_{12}+\ell_{22}\implies \ell_{22}=a_{22}-\ell_{21}u_{12} \] \[ \ell_{22}=-8-12\left(-\frac{1}{3}\right)=-8+4=-4 \] \[ a_{23}=\ell_{21}u_{13}+\ell_{22}u_{23}\implies u_{23}=\frac{a_{23}-\ell_{21}u_{13}}{\ell_{22}} \] \[ u_{23}=\frac{6-12\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)}{-4} =\frac{6-4}{-4} =\frac{2}{-4} =-\frac{1}{2} \] \[ a_{24}=\ell_{21}u_{14}+\ell_{22}u_{24}\implies u_{24}=\frac{a_{24}-\ell_{21}u_{14}}{\ell_{22}} \] \[ u_{24}=\frac{10-12\left(\frac{2}{3}\right)}{-4} =\frac{10-8}{-4} =\frac{2}{-4} =-\frac{1}{2} \]

Step 4: Third column of \(L\) and \(u_{34}\)

\[ a_{32}=\ell_{31}u_{12}+\ell_{32}\implies \ell_{32}=a_{32}-\ell_{31}u_{12} \] \[ \ell_{32}=-13-3\left(-\frac{1}{3}\right)=-13+1=-12 \] \[ a_{33}=\ell_{31}u_{13}+\ell_{32}u_{23}+\ell_{33}\implies \ell_{33}=a_{33}-\left(\ell_{31}u_{13}+\ell_{32}u_{23}\right) \] \[ \ell_{33}=9-\left(3\cdot\frac{1}{3}+(-12)\cdot\left(-\frac{1}{2}\right)\right) =9-(1+6)=2 \] \[ a_{34}=\ell_{31}u_{14}+\ell_{32}u_{24}+\ell_{33}u_{34}\implies u_{34}=\frac{a_{34}-\left(\ell_{31}u_{14}+\ell_{32}u_{24}\right)}{\ell_{33}} \] \[ u_{34}=\frac{3-\left(3\cdot\frac{2}{3}+(-12)\cdot\left(-\frac{1}{2}\right)\right)}{2} =\frac{3-(2+6)}{2} =\frac{-5}{2} =-\frac{5}{2} \]

Step 5: Finish last row of \(L\)

\[ a_{42}=\ell_{41}u_{12}+\ell_{42}\implies \ell_{42}=a_{42}-\ell_{41}u_{12} \] \[ \ell_{42}=4-(-6)\left(-\frac{1}{3}\right)=4-2=2 \] \[ a_{43}=\ell_{41}u_{13}+\ell_{42}u_{23}+\ell_{43}\implies \ell_{43}=a_{43}-\left(\ell_{41}u_{13}+\ell_{42}u_{23}\right) \] \[ \ell_{43}=1-\left((-6)\cdot\frac{1}{3}+2\cdot\left(-\frac{1}{2}\right)\right) =1-(-2-1)=4 \] \[ a_{44}=\ell_{41}u_{14}+\ell_{42}u_{24}+\ell_{43}u_{34}+\ell_{44} \implies \ell_{44}=a_{44}-\left(\ell_{41}u_{14}+\ell_{42}u_{24}+\ell_{43}u_{34}\right) \] \[ \ell_{44}=-18-\left((-6)\cdot\frac{2}{3}+2\cdot\left(-\frac{1}{2}\right)+4\cdot\left(-\frac{5}{2}\right)\right) \] \[ \ell_{44}=-18-\left(-4-1-10\right)=-18-(-15)=-3 \]

Thus, the Crout decomposition is

\[ A=LU \] \[ L= \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 0 & 0 & 0\\ 12 & -4 & 0 & 0\\ 3 & -12 & 2 & 0\\ -6 & 2 & 4 & -3 \end{bmatrix}, \qquad U= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -\frac{1}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3}\\ 0 & 1 & -\frac{1}{2} & -\frac{1}{2}\\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -\frac{5}{2}\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]
