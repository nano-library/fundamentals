Problem Sheet 1

1.

(i) Draw the electric field profile of a fundamental mode of a planar optical waveguide.

(ii) What is the cut-off condition of a mode? What is the physical understanding of the mode cutoff?

(iii) Suppose you have fabricated an optical waveguide, which is single-moded at \( 1.55 \mu\text{m} \) wavelength. How do you operate this waveguide in the multimode regime?

2.

(i) Show that a guided wave in a step index optical waveguide corresponds to a superposition of two plane waves propagating at a particular angle with the \( z \)-axis.

(ii) For a waveguide with a core refractive index of \( 1.5 \) and a cladding refractive index of \( 1.48 \), if we operate at \( 1.06 \mu\text{m} \), what would be the maximum width of the core for it to be single-moded?

(iii) Suppose a planar waveguide satisfies the following condition for the \( V \)-number, estimate how many symmetric and anti-symmetric modes can be supported.

\[ 2m\pi < V < (2m+1)\pi \]

3.

(i) Draw a schematic of an optical directional coupler.

(ii) Write two applications of an optical directional coupler.

(iii) Write down the parameters that control the coupling coefficient in a directional coupler.

(iv) What are quarter-wave and half-wave plates?
