10. Optical Directional Coupler

COUPLING FACTORS

  • Separation between waveguides
  • Propagation constants
  • Operating wavelength

Consider two independent waveguides supporting modes \(a(z)\) and \(b(z)\).

For waveguide 1:

\[ E_y(x,z)\propto e^{-i\beta_1 z} \] \[ \frac{da}{dz}=-i\beta_1 a \tag{10.1} \]

For waveguide 2:

\[ \frac{db}{dz}=-i\beta_2 b \tag{10.2} \]

COUPLED MODE EQUATIONS

When the waveguides are brought close, evanescent fields overlap and coupling occurs:

\[ \frac{da}{dz}=-i\beta_1 a - i\kappa_{12} b \tag{10.3} \] \[ \frac{db}{dz}=-i\beta_2 b - i\kappa_{21} a \tag{10.4} \]

\(\kappa_{12},\kappa_{21}\) are coupling coefficients. For identical waveguides: \(\kappa_{12}=\kappa_{21}=\kappa\).

EIGENMODE SOLUTION

Assume solutions of the form: \[ a(z)=a_0 e^{-i\beta z} \tag{10.5} \] \[ b(z)=b_0 e^{-i\beta z} \tag{10.6} \]

Substitution into Equations (10.3) and (10.4) gives:

\[ a_0(\beta-\beta_1)-\kappa_{12} b_0=0 \tag{10.7} \] \[ b_0(\beta-\beta_2)-\kappa_{21} a_0=0 \tag{10.8} \]

For non-trivial solutions:

\[ \beta^2-\beta(\beta_1+\beta_2)+(\beta_1\beta_2-\kappa^2)=0 \tag{10.9} \]

where

\[ \kappa=\sqrt{\kappa_{12}\kappa_{21}}. \] Thus the propagation constants of the symmetric and antisymmetric supermodes are: \[ \beta_{s,a}=\frac{\beta_1+\beta_2}{2} \pm \sqrt{\frac{(\beta_1-\beta_2)^2}{4}+\kappa^2} \tag{10.10} \]

Identical Waveguides

If \(\beta_1=\beta_2=\beta_0\): \[ \beta_s=\beta_0+\kappa \tag{10.11} \] \[ \beta_a=\beta_0-\kappa \tag{10.12} \]

Power Exchange

For excitation in waveguide 1 at \(z=0\): \[ |a(z)|^2= 1-\frac{\kappa^2}{\frac{\Delta\beta^2}{4}+\kappa^2} \sin^2\!\left( \sqrt{\frac{\Delta\beta^2}{4}+\kappa^2}\; z \right) \tag{10.13} \] \[ |b(z)|^2= \frac{\kappa^2}{\frac{\Delta\beta^2}{4}+\kappa^2} \sin^2\!\left( \sqrt{\frac{\Delta\beta^2}{4}+\kappa^2}\; z \right) \tag{10.14} \]

where

\[ \Delta\beta=\beta_1-\beta_2. \]

Power conservation:

\[ |a(z)|^2+|b(z)|^2=1. \tag{10.15} \]

Modulation Length

\[ h=\frac{2\pi}{2\sqrt{\frac{\Delta\beta^2}{4}+\kappa^2}} \tag{10.16} \]

Coupling Length

Minimum length for maximum power transfer: \[ L_c=\frac{h}{2} = \frac{\pi}{2\sqrt{\frac{\Delta\beta^2}{4}+\kappa^2}} \tag{10.17} \]

For identical waveguides (\(\Delta\beta=0\)):

\[ L_c=\frac{\pi}{2\kappa} \tag{10.18} \]
