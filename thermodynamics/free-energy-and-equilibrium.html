Free Energy and Equilibrium

\[ dS_{\text{total}} = -\frac{1}{T}dF \] An increase in the total entropy of the universe is the same as the decreases in the Helmholtz's free energy. If the system $(T,V,N)$ constant, system always tries to minimize its Helmholtz's free energy. \[ dS_{\text{total}} = -\frac{1}{T}dG_{\text{system}} \] An increase in the total entropy of the universe is the same as the decreases in the Gibb's free energy of the system.
